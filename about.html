About Vedic-Spark

Our Mission


Vedic Spark is dedicated to revolutionizing traditional practices through innovative technology. Specializing in metal and mineral merchant wholesaling, we seamlessly blend heritage with modern advances. By integrating electronics with time-honored traditions, like our flagship product, the Automated Dip Prajwalan, we make cultural heritage more accessible and relevant in today's fast- paced world. Our mission is to preserve the essence of tradition while enhancing the convenience and functionality of these practices for future generations.


Our Vision


To become a global leader in the automation of traditional practices, ensuring that cultural heritage is preserved while embracing the advancements of the modern world.
